FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before subscribing.

What is Kick Launcher?
Kick Launcher is a Windows desktop app that automatically captures clips from your Kick stream, edits them with AI captions and your branding, and posts them to TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, and YouTube — all without you doing anything manually.
How do I get my activation key after subscribing?
After your payment is confirmed, your unique activation key will be emailed to you within minutes. Paste it into the Kick Launcher activation screen to unlock the app.
What happens when my subscription expires?
Kick Launcher will show a subscription expired screen when you open it. Simply renew your subscription and you'll receive a new key to re-activate. All your settings and connected accounts are preserved.
Does it work with Twitch and YouTube Live too?
Yes! Kick Launcher can monitor Kick, Twitch, and YouTube Live simultaneously. Kick is the primary focus but multi-platform stream monitoring is built in.
What are the system requirements?
Kick Launcher runs on Windows 10/11 (64-bit). You need Python 3.11+ installed, or use the standalone .exe version that requires no Python. A stable internet connection is required for uploading clips.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, cancel anytime. Your subscription key stays active until the end of the billing period you already paid for. No refunds on partial months, but you keep access until expiry.
Is my social media data safe?
Yes. Kick Launcher stores all your API credentials locally on your own computer in a config file. Nothing is sent to our servers. You control your own data at all times.
